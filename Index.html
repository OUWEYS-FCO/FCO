About Us

FCO was born on September 12, 2023 with a clear vision: to create affordable and revolutionary technology

Young Innovators

We are a team of 50 engineers passionate about technology, founded by young visionaries. Although we are new to the market, our determination and creativity make us a force to be reckoned with.

Our mission is simple: create technological products that rival those from Apple and Samsung, but at more accessible prices and with innovative design.

We believe that age is not a limit for innovation. With fresh ideas and determination, we are ready to compete with technology giants.
